Cayarani Rural Sanitation and Potable Water Project

Province of Condesuyos, Arequipa - Project ID: CUI 2619605

Overview Problem Statement Objectives Solution Financial Impact Project Document

Executive Summary

Overview

This project seeks to establish a sustainable rural water supply and sanitation system in the annexes of Choq√±ihuaqui, Arcata, and Umachulco within the district of Cayarani, addressing the lack of clean water and sanitation services.

Budget: S/. 3,387,250.64

Execution Timeline: 6 months, with a 20-year sustainability plan

Problem Statement

Lack of access to clean water and sanitation has led to high rates of waterborne diseases and inadequate hygiene practices across affected communities.

    High prevalence of gastrointestinal diseases
    Unsafe water sources and reliance on untreated sources
    Poor sanitation infrastructure

Project Objectives

Objectives
    Provide clean and reliable water sources
    Improve health and reduce disease rates
    Develop sustainable infrastructure for long-term use

Technical Solution

Key Components
    Water Supply: Installation of 74 spring capture systems and pipelines across communities
    Sanitation Units: 77 basic sanitation units with improved septic systems
    Distribution Network: Piping infrastructure to deliver clean water to each household

Financial Overview

Budget and Maintenance

Total Cost: S/. 3,387,250.64

Annual Maintenance Cost: S/. 8,312.48

This budget covers the full construction and long-term maintenance needs, ensuring sustainability for 20 years.

Environmental Impact and Sustainability

    The project incorporates environmentally friendly practices to minimize ecological impact, such as, safe waste management, and community education on sustainable water usage
      To ensure that the project delivers the intended results throughout its lifespan, timely availability of resources for operation and maintenance is being considered. The administration of operations and maintenance will be directly managed by the District Municipality of Cayarani and/or the corresponding JASS, along with the necessary institutional arrangements for both the execution and operational phases.
