Our Commitment to Sustainability

Our name, inspired by the Quechua word "huerta" (vegetable garden), pays tribute to the Andean tradition of cultivating and sharing with loved ones, keeping our roots and heritage alive.

La Huerta Refugio is dedicated to preserving Andean heritage, supporting local economic growth, and fostering sustainable tourism. We are a locally owned business, deeply rooted in our agricultural and Andean heritage.

We take pride in sharing our culture and fostering educational exchanges. Additionally, by partnering with local families and businesses, we emphasize the importance of community economic development and sustainable tourism.
