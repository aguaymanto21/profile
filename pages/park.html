Project Overview

This project will create inclusive public spaces in Aymaña to enhance community integration, improve quality of life, and foster social interaction.

Budget: S/. 1,068,341.45

Duration: 4 months, beginning in September 2023

Objectives

  • Establish safe, accessible public spaces for community interaction.
  • Promote social integration and support mental well-being.
  • Encourage physical activity and recreational use of community areas.

Infrastructure Details

The project covers an area of 4,939.8 m2 and includes:

Recreational Zones

Includes sports facilities and play areas for all age groups.

Safety Features

Perimeter fencing, lighting, and security stations.

Green Spaces

Landscaped areas with benches and pathways.

Social Benefits

  • Promotes a sense of community and cultural identity.
  • Encourages healthier lifestyles through physical activity.
  • Boosts the local economy through events and fairs.

Environmental Impact

This project will prioritize sustainability with features like:

  • Dedicated green spaces and natural areas.
  • Eco-friendly waste management systems.
  • Low-impact lighting and energy-efficient design.
